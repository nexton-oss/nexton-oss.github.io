NEXTON Open Source Software

A curated collection of home-grown repositories from NEXTON covering Machine Learning, Generative AI and Agentic AI for decision intelligence, local AI for developers and AI practitioners. The goal is simple: publish useful and technically serious work that helps teams build better software and more trustworthy systems.

All these projects originate from real engineering needs encountered with our clients and internal teams.

NEXTON publishes selected open-source assets to demonstrate engineering standards, share reusable building blocks and promote a responsible approach to AI systems.

We prefer trust by design over trust by claim or certification alone. In practice, this means exposing the least possible: the least possible client-specific material, the least possible sensitive information and the least possible operational risk.

Every repository is deliberately selected, clearly owned and published only after review and usage when it reflects the engineering standards NEXTON chooses to stand behind.
